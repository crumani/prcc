Welcome to Printer's Row Coffee Co.

Hello! We are Printer's Row Coffee Company: a Chicago based small-batch coffee roasting company.

Our cafe and roastery is located at 2482 N. Lincoln Avenue in Lincoln Park. We are currently open everyday from 7AM - 1PM for takeaway only. Our 750 sq. ft. space is equal parts cafe and production space.

We pride ourselves in being a beacon in the Lincoln Park community, serving handcrafted coffee in small batches to give you the best that each bean has to offer. Come visit us soon and grab a drink or some coffee beans, and wear your mask!
